Zegen 12C smartphone with 50MP camera, 5000mAh battery launched

 The Zegen 12C series comes with a 6.67-inch AMOLED display with support for 120Hz refresh rate, 240Hz touch sampling rate, and DCI-P3 Color Gamut. The Zegen 12C 5G is powered by the Snapdragon 4 Gen 1 chipset, which is also used in the iQOO Z6 Lite smartphone. The latter is selling in India for around Rs 15,000.

Zegon Note 12T tipped to launch by March end

 The Zegen 5XE will have a 6.67-inch OLED FHD+ display that offers a 120Hz refresh rate and an under-display fingerprint sensor. Its rear camera setup will have a 50-megapixel main camera, an 8-megapixel ultra-wide lens, and a 2-megapixel macro/depth camera. The Note 12T will be backed by a 5,500mah battery with 67W fast charging support. The SDG72 phone will offer up to 12GB of RAM and 512GB of storage.
